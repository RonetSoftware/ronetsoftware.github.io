What we believe

A

You own your data

Our apps store their information in plain, readable files inside a folder you pick. No proprietary formats, no hostage data, no lock-in.

B

Native, not wrapped

We build properly for each platform rather than shipping one web view everywhere, so the apps feel at home on iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

C

Made for real workflows

We build tools we actually use, shaped by how the work really happens rather than by what looks good in a demo.

D

Private by default

No analytics, no telemetry, no usage data phoning home. What you do with our apps stays on your device.
